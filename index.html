About Us

We are a Healthcare Consulting firm with a focus on strategic partnerships, business development, management consulting, practice development and other related aspects in the healthcare field.

Our Focus

Caliber Partners uses its knowledge, experience and powerful network of consultants, senior executives, key healthcare thought-leaders and strategic investment partners to help our clients achieve their goals. We focus on two client sectors: industry partners and healthcare providers.

Industry Partnerships

Our partners usually have new and/or emerging healthcare technologies. We help our clients by identifying and securing strategic investment opportunities, by generating revenue through sales and business development efforts, by securing licensing and partnership opportunities, by providing access to key thought leaders and through traditional management consulting.

Healthcare Providers

We partner with healthcare providers through practice development, management consulting and other related areas including launching commercial ventures. We have extensive experience and expertise in several practice areas including orthopedics, neurosurgery, neurology, radiology as well as ambulatory surgery centers (ASCs) and specialty hospitals.
